About SafeBite

A specialized platform for food safety analysis and allergy prevention.

💡 Our Idea

SafeBite is a digital companion designed to help users analyze food products and ingredients based on their specific allergy profiles. Our platform focuses on providing accurate data to ensure safer dietary choices, featuring a custom AI assistant specifically trained to answer your food-related questions.

🎯 Our Goals

  • Simplify the process of reading and understanding food labels.
  • Reduce the risk of accidental exposure to allergens.
  • Provide a reliable database of ingredients and their safety levels.
  • Empower users with a dedicated AI assistant for instant support.
